About Penny Juice

Penny Juice provides colorful, healthy juice concentrates designed especially for childcare centers. Our mission is to make nutrition simple, fun, and affordable for kids across the country.

We offer a wide variety of flavors, all made without added sugar, preservatives, or artificial sweeteners. Each bottle is easy to mix, easy to store, and perfect for busy childcare environments.

At Penny Juice, we believe every child deserves great taste and great nutrition—one cup at a time.

Our Commitment

We are committed to helping organizations provide better beverage options for children. By focusing on quality, value, and great taste, Penny Juice continues to serve communities across the country.
